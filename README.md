# Cloud Infra

Working at Meta and Uber, it was interesting to see how bigger, established companies handle their infrastructure. Both had internal paradigms and frameworks for managing their CI/CD and the server racks that power their business. There was, however, one instance at Meta where I actually got to leverage AWS over Meta's on-prem capabilities. My work there captured something called IAC which was a relatively new concept to me but made soooooo much sense when I first heard about it. Infrastructure-as-code comes in different flavors, but the one I'm most familiar with is Hashicorp's open-sourced [terraform](https://github.com/hashicorp/terraform). I like that IAC enables me to "rollback" some erroneous cloud changes. Here, you'll see me manage my personal projects here using Terraform. My hope is it could be a guide to either a future me or others intrigued by work and wanting to replicate it.
